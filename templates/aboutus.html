About Us


"The most popular venue... Fine... The most popular venue, aside from the tavern, in the whole city of Mondstadt is Good Hunter. It is always filled with mouthwatering aroma and a great atmosphere. If you can't get your reservations, at least ordering take-out is still an option."

From the local cuisine of the city Mondstadt, the Li and Yue style of cooking from Liyue, to the ice-cold fresh food and seafood from Inazuma, Good Hunter boasts it's wide selection of dishes for everyone to try! In The Good Hunter, there is food for everyone!



Sara Musk

Owner

The Head Chef at Good Hunter and also a waitress there, Sara is extremely passionate about cooking and excels at the different cuisines from Mondstadt, Liyue, and Inazuma. Sara is a true master of the culinary arts with all the skills of a kitchen veteran. She enjoys a good reputation among the hearty eaters in Mondstadt. There's absolutely no need to be nervous if she wants you to sample her latest creation. It will not disappoint. Promise.
